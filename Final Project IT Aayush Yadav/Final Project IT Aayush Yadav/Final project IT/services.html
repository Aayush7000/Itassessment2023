Good Designs for Good People

Space Planning

Space planning is a fundamental element of the interior design process. It is with an in-depth analysis of how space is to fill the purpose of every area. The space plan will also define the circulation patterns that show how people will move through space. The designer defines the spaces with the furniture and the activities that will take place in the zone. It involves creating a space plan, a drawing that shows the arrangement of functional elements within a space.

Interior architecture

Interior architecture is the design of a building or shelter from inside out or the design of a new interior for a type of home that can be fixed. Interior architecture is the design of an interior in architectural terms. It is the use and reuse of the space. It is the process by which the interiors are designed to address all aspects of the home and the way is used.
