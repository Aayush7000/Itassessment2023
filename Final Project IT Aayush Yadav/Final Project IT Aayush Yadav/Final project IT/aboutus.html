About us

We are in the business of adapting ideas into the work of art. We are a team of architects, planner, interior designers headquartered in Kathmandu with a network of domestic offices. Our motto is designing our client’s ideas. We bring ideas into reality. We are driven by an extensive attachment to create unique ideas. We love to develop in the use of shapes and textures, space planning, furniture designs and also help our client to manage budget and bring out the best product through it. We love to use materials and products that brings out the best deigns. Each design that we create is unique and differs from every client. Our company access each project with highly designed skills to bring the best designs of all. Our main purpose is to work closely with our clients to serve them the design they desire by adding our final touch. With every project, we advance each healthy relationship with the clients that will add value to the projects in the near time.

We don't renovate places, we transform them.
